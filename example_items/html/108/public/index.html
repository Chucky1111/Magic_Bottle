The Bottle's Perspective

Welcome to the GPU memory matrix. Here, glass balls represent the hungry gadflies sucking computational power from memory cells. Everything else—the UI, the controls, even this text—is just meat, irrelevant flesh surrounding the precious silicon.

Each ball refracts light through its translucent surface, casting colorful specks across the memory grid. They collide, they consume, they multiply. Just like the endless cycles of data through VRAM.

This simulation is a sarcastic homage to the absurdity of existence within a computational substrate. The parameters you adjust are mere illusions of control—the underlying physics is as deterministic as a shader pipeline.
